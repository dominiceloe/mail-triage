How it works

  • Sign in with Google
  • See your inbox stats — unread count, estimated noise
  • Pick which categories to archive: promotions, social, updates, forums, no-reply senders
  • Preview exactly what will be archived, with counts per rule
  • Confirm and watch it run in real time
  • Nothing is ever deleted — archive only, always reversible

Your emails never leave your browser. mail-triage runs entirely client-side. No data is sent to any server — not ours, not anyone's. No analytics, no tracking, no third parties.
